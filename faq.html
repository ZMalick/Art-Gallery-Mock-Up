Frequently Asked Questions

For Visitors

We feature a diverse collection of original artwork including oil and watercolor paintings, bronze and marble sculptures, and charcoal, graphite, and ink sketches. Every piece in our gallery is an original work — we do not carry reproductions or prints.

Yes, we welcome visitors to our Austin, Texas gallery. Our hours are Tuesday through Saturday from 10am to 6pm, and Sunday from 12pm to 5pm. We are closed on Mondays. No appointment is necessary, but feel free to contact us if you'd like a guided tour.

Each artwork on our website has a dedicated detail page with information about the piece, its medium, dimensions, and the artist who created it. If you have additional questions, you can reach out to us through our contact form and we'll be happy to help.

We periodically host artist receptions, exhibition openings, and community events at our gallery. Follow us on social media or check back on our website for announcements about upcoming events.

For Artists

We accept submissions through our contact form. Select "Artist Application" as the subject and include a brief artist statement along with links to images of your work. Our curatorial team reviews every submission and will respond within two to three weeks.

We look for original artwork that demonstrates strong craftsmanship, a unique artistic voice, and professional presentation. We accept paintings, sculptures, and sketches in a variety of media. Visit our consignment page for more details on our criteria.

Our consignment process has three steps: you submit your work, our team reviews it, and if accepted, your artwork is featured in our gallery and on our website. We handle all aspects of presentation and display. Visit our consignment page for a full overview.

Not at all. While we are based in Austin, Texas, we represent artists from across the country and internationally. We work with artists to coordinate the logistics of displaying and managing their work in our gallery.
